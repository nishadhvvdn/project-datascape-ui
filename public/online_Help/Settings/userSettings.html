Saving the Settings

After changing the settings to your desired state, they may be saved by clicking the "Original View" button at the top right of the screen. Put a name for your new settings in the "name this view" text box, click the "make it default" checkbox, and then click the save button to set the new default settings.

Temperature Unit Selection

To change the default temperature type displayed throughout the system from Celsius to Farhenheit or vice versa, just click the dropdown under user settings labeled "temperature" and select the desired setting.

If the report has a large amount of data, it might separate into pages. In this case, you can choose how many entries to display, or which page to show, from the pagination tool bar in the bottom left of the report.

Currency Unit Selection

To change the default currency type displayed throughout the system, just click the dropdown next to "amount per unit" to the desired setting. To change the cost per 1 KW (this is used when displaying a monetary value), simply type a new number into the text box next to the dropdown.
