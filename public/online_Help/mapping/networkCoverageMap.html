Map Navigation

The summary map contains several different device layers. The first view presented will be of all the DTCs in the system. To view any transformers connected to one of the shown DTCs, simply click the related DTC icon. You can then see what HyperHUBs™ are connected to the transformer's HyperSPROUT™ by clicking on the transformer, and finally, what meters are connected to that transformer by clicking any of the HyperHUBs™.

To go back a layer, just click the back button in the top left corner of the mapping screen.

Navigation Tools

Every map will display a small vertical bar in the top left corner of the map while you have the cursor over the map. From the top, down, the toolbar allows you to search for a specific city, zoom in and out, return to the original display before you started moving around the map, and drag/select.

Understanding the Info Boxes

Each level of the map has an info box relating to what devices the map is showing. It will change in accordance with what icons are visible on the map (see the next list item for more information on changing visible devices). Each possible item in the info boxes is described in the following list.

  • 1. Total DTC: The total DTCs in the system
  • 2. Connected DTC: The total DTCs currently visible
  • 3. Connected Transformers: The total transformers currently visible
  • 4. Connected Meters: The total meters currently visible or connected to the currently visible devices
  • 5. DTC ID: The ID of the DTC associated with the displayed devices
  • 6. HyperSPROUT™ ID: the ID of the HyperSPROUT™ associated with the displayed devices
  • 7. Transformer ID: The ID of the transformer associated with the displayed devices
  • 8. Meter ID: The serial number of the meter associated with the displayed device
  • 9. Meter Device ID: The ID of the meter associated with the displayed device
  • 10. Latitude: The latitude of the transformer or meter, depending on what was last clicked
  • 11. Longitude: The longitude of the transformer or meter, depending on what last clicked
  • 12. Top Oil Temp: The oil temperature at the top of the transformer that was last clicked
  • 13. Bottom Oil Temp: The oil temperature at the bottom of the transformer that was last clicked
  • 14. 24h Power Flow (kW): The total kW sent into the devices connected to the displayed devices
  • 15. Average kWh: The average kW usage per hour by the devices connected to the displayed devices
  • 16. 24h Return (kW): The total kW that has been returned to the grid by the devices connected to the displayed devices
  • 17. Average Return kWh: The average kW returned per hour by the devices connected to the displayed devices
  • 18. Last Refreshed Date Time: The last time the information displayed was refreshed There is also an Errors and Warnings box displayed below each of the info boxes. This box will display the number of errors being reported for the displayed devices.

Device Control

To modify what devices are shown on the map, simply click the "Select Device" (device type will match whatever devices are being shown on the map) dropdown and select the devices you wish to be seen.

Viewing the Response Rate

On each page there will be a network response rate box. This box will show the percentage of visible devices that responded when queried each hour. This graph can also be expanded to a full screen size by clicking the "Show Details" button. Scrolling over any point in the line will give details on that specific data point.
