Uploading a Bill

To upload a billing input file (this is used to identify what meters to pull readings from and their account information), just click the "Upload File" button. Then, choose the input to upload by clicking the "choose file" button in the resulting window. After clicking import the file will be imported and the meters in the upload file will appear in the list below.

Reading the Report

The billing report shows the uploaded bill (or nothing if no bill has been uploaded yet). The report contains eight columns.

  • 1. Account: Taken from the uploaded input file, this shows theaccount number for the meter in the client's billing system.
  • 2. Physical Mtr No: The serial number of the meter
  • 3. Old Reading: The last kw reading made on the meter
  • 4. Old Date: The date the last reading was made
  • 5. New Reading: The newest kw reading the system could pull
  • 6. New Date: The date the newest reading was pulled from
  • 7. Latitude: The latitude where the meter is located
  • 8. Longitude: The longitude where the meter is located

If the report has a large amount of data, it might separate into pages. In this case, you can choose how many entries to display, or which page to show, from the pagination tool bar in the bottom left of the report.

Sorting the Report

To sort the report, click the header of whichever column you would like to sort by. For instance, clicking on the Account number header will switch the column between the default display, ascending account numbers, and descending account numbers

Exporting a Report

The report gives four different options for exporting. You may export either all the data in the report or just the currently displayed data, in either a CSV format or a PDF format. To do this, simply click on the export button in the top right corner of the report after filtering it for the desired data.

Exporting an Old Report

To export an old billing report (i.e. a report with a new reading date of your choosing rather then the newest available) click the "Export File" button. Then put in a time for when the new reading should be taken from and click export.
