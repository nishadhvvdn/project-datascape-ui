Creating a Report

To create a report, begin by choosing a timeframe by clicking on the time picker, choosing a date, an hour, and the minute. Then, after filling this in, click the "Get Data" button. The report will instantly begin to load.

Reading the Report

The non-technical loss report shows the amount of loss on transformers expected of having a meter involved with theft. The report contains seven columns.

  • 1. DTC ID: The ID of the DTC where the transformer is grouped
  • 2. Affected Transformers: The ID of the transformer affected by the non-technical loss
  • 3. Connected Devices: The meters connected to the transformer
  • 4. Event Identify Time: The timestamp for when the loss first began recording
  • 5. Total KW Sent: The total KW sent to the meters
  • 6. Total KW Recorded: The total KW returned by the meters
  • 7. Delta: The delta between the sent and recorded KW readings

If the report has a large amount of data, it might separate into pages. In this case, you can choose how many entries to display, or which page to show, from the pagination tool bar in the bottom left of the report.

Sorting the Report

To sort the report, click the header of whichever column you would like to sort by. For instance, clicking on the DTC ID header will switch the column between the default display, ascending, and descending.

Filtering the Report

The report gives four different options for exporting. You may export either all the data in the report or just the currently displayed data, in either a CSV format or a PDF format. To do this simply click on the export button in the top right corner of the report after filtering it for the desired data.
